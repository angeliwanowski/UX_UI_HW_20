AirPortal

Airports made easy

This case study eases stress and confusion during airport travel. AirPortal is an interactive airport map providing ETA for travel to gate, flight changes, and a TSA wait time.


project overview:

the problem:

Travelers struggle to navigate unfamiliar airports, often leading to costly and stressful situations.

the solution:

AirPortal is an interactive airport map that aids travelers around the world. This mobile tool offers solutions to flight changes and airport navigation.

my role:

UX Designer and Researcher

responsibilities:

  • user research
  • solution ideation
  • mobile prototyping
  • ui design
  • wireframing
  • usability testing
  • design theory
  • competitor analysis


user research:

5 users interviewed
3 primary objectives:

  • How users feel during booking process
  • How users feel while navigating the airport
  • How users feel while navigating their destination

research findings:

Users expressed the following concerns regarding the airport:

“One of my biggest fears of traveling is:
What if I get lost at the airport?
What if I miss my flight.”
“Sometimes they don’t even announce
gate changes.”

80%

of users described stress while within the airport.

missing her flight.

brainstorming:

feature prioritization:

wireframing:

"lo-fi":

I began by hand drawing wireframe ideas. I drew inspiration from successful and established navigation apps (Waze, Google Maps, Apple Maps).
There were several standardized tactics:

  • ETA and directions at the bottom of screen
  • menu icons in a top nav bar
  • symbol to represent current location

"mid-fi":

I then digitized my wireframes and began building out the functionality.
I adhered to some of Apple’s design guidelines:

  • SF Pro typography
  • alert colors
  • button styles

testing plan:

10 users tested
3 tasks:

  • Sign up using Google credentials
  • Use AirPortal to find the nearest bathroom
  • Find information on a terminal restaurant

100%

of users successfully completed these tasks.

user feedback:

positive:

  • Layout is similar to other navigational apps
  • Visually simplistic
  • User friendly

critical:

  • Certain icons were confusing
  • Tutorial is imperceptible
  • Make gate ETA tool automatic

iterations:

improved visibility of tutorial
